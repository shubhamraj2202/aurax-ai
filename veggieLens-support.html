How do I scan food with VeggieLens?
Simply open the app, point your camera at the food or ingredient list, and tap the scan button. You can also select photos from your gallery. Our AI will analyze the image and tell you if it's vegan or vegetarian.
How accurate is the AI analysis?
VeggieLens uses advanced AI technology for high accuracy, but results are predictions. For critical dietary decisions, always verify ingredients independently.

What's included in the free version?
Free users get 10 scans per month plus 1 daily scan after reaching the monthly limit. You can also earn additional free scans by watching optional ads.
What do I get with VeggieLens Pro?
Pro subscribers enjoy unlimited scans with no restrictions. Perfect for frequent food scanners and those with specific dietary needs.
How do I restore my purchase?
Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous subscriptions linked to your Apple ID.

What happens to my photos?
Images are sent to Google's AI for analysis but are not stored by VeggieLens or Google. Your scan history is saved locally on your device only.
Can I delete my scan history?
Yes! Your scan history is stored locally on your device. You can delete individual scans in the History tab or remove all data by deleting the app.
